Our Story

Rooted in Raleigh, grown with care.

Our mission

Sprout & Grow is a local greenhouse nursery committed to helping Raleigh gardeners create healthy, sustainable spaces. Every plant in our greenhouse is meticulously nurtured using organic practices, so you can feel confident about what you bring into your home, yard, balcony, or community garden.

We believe gardening should feel approachable, rewarding, and connected to the natural world. That is why our team offers personalized, friendly guidance alongside every plant we sell — not just a transaction, but a relationship with your garden.

Our commitment to organic growing is part of a larger goal: reducing our ecological footprint and helping build a greener Raleigh, one garden at a time. To see how organic standards are defined nationally, visit the USDA's guide to organic agriculture.

What we stand for

  • Transparency — we share exactly how our plants are grown, from seed to sale.
  • Integrity — honest advice, even if that means telling you a plant isn't right for your space.
  • Environmental stewardship — organic practices that protect pollinators, soil, and water.
  • Community — workshops and events that help Raleigh gardeners learn and grow together.
