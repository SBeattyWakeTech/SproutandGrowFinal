Organic plants, thoughtfully grown

Sprout & Grow is a local greenhouse nursery committed to helping Raleigh gardeners create healthy, sustainable spaces. Every plant is carefully nurtured using organic practices, so you can feel confident about what you bring into your home, yard, balcony, or community garden.

Whether you are starting your first container garden or planning a full backyard refresh, our knowledgeable team can help you choose plants that fit your space, light, and experience level. We believe gardening should feel approachable, rewarding, and connected to the natural world.

Learn more about sustainable gardening through the NC State Extension Gardener Handbook, a trusted resource for North Carolina growers.
