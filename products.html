Choosing the right plant

Not sure where to start? Consider these questions before you shop:

  1. How much direct sunlight does the space get each day?
  2. Will the plant live indoors, outdoors, or on a balcony?
  3. How much time do you want to spend on watering and care?
  4. Are you growing for beauty, food, or pollinators?
